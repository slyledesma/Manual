# Manual of me

👋 About Me

Hi! I’m Leonardo, an Agile Coach with a strong background as a Business Analyst (8+ years). I enjoy helping teams improve the way they work, deliver value, and continuously evolve. I’m also happy to jump into business discussions when needed.

🕒 Working Hours

Typically: 8:30 – 17:30
I’m flexible when needed, so feel free to reach out outside these hours if something important comes up.

📅 Meetings & Collaboration

I usually have lunch between 13:00 and 14:00, so I really appreciate avoiding meetings during that time unless there’s no other option.
I tend to prefer meetings in the morning, but afternoons work perfectly fine as well.
I value meetings that bring clear outcomes—if something can be handled asynchronously, I generally prefer that.

Tip:
If you’re scheduling a meeting and find a free slot in my calendar, feel free to book it 👍
Adding a short agenda or context in the invite is highly appreciated—it helps everyone make the most of the time.

💬 Communication Style

Slack is the best way to reach me (faster than email or calls).
Feel free to be direct and to the point, I appreciate clarity.
That said, starting with a simple “Hi! How are you?” is always welcome 🙂

🌍 Languages

We can have meetings in whichever language is more comfortable for you.

⚙️ Ways of Working

I’m comfortable with meetings when they add value, but I lean toward async communication when possible.
I believe in continuous improvement, transparency, and collaboration.
I enjoy working closely with both teams and stakeholders to align on goals and outcomes.
